Creed & Theology All Levels

Aqeedah

This circle explores Aqeedah not as a fixed definition but as a living framework, grounded in how the Qur'an speaks directly to "those who have believed." Each session traces how belief is tied to accountability — through commands, prohibitions, and corrections that follow the Qur'an's address to believers. Foundational concepts of Aqeedah and Iman are revisited and refined across sessions, building a grounded, responsible understanding of belief rather than one that is simply inherited or assumed.

Ongoing Sat, 7:00 PM - 10:30 PM All Levels

Topics Covered:

  • The Qur'anic address "O you who have believed" and its significance
  • Foundations and definitions of Aqeedah
  • Foundations and definitions of Iman

Prerequisites: None — open to all students seeking a grounded study of belief.

Islamic Jurisprudence Intermediate to Advanced

Al Fiqh Al Shamil

This advanced course is for the serious student of Islamic knowledge. Through a deep, hadith-by-hadith study of Sahih Bukhari — the most authentic book after the Quran — students explore the rulings, wisdom, and spiritual lessons embedded in prophetic tradition. Each hadith is examined with its full context, Arabic breakdown, legal rulings, and spiritual benefits. This is not a surface-level study. It is a scholarly experience designed for those who are ready to go deeper — spiritually, mentally, and in every aspect of their lives.

Ongoing Thur, 7:30 PM - 10:30 PM Intermediate to Advanced

Prerequisites: Sciences of the Quran

Quranic Studies All Levels

Ahl Al Quran

Ahlul Quran — the People of the Quran — is a structured and community-driven memorization program designed for those who have completed the Islamic & Arabic Grammar Course. In this class, every student is assigned a partner and together they are given five Juz of the Quran to memorize, building accountability and brotherhood/sisterhood into the journey from the very start. Progress is structured at two pages per week, with individualized schedules available to accommodate each student’s pace and goals. Classes are held every Monday, and the experience goes beyond memorization — students recite to one another, reinforcing the Tajweed rules mastered in the prerequisite course and elevating their recitation from intermediate to true mastery. This is where knowledge becomes practice, and practice becomes a way of life.

Ongoing Friday All Levels

Prerequisites: Tajweed / Quranic Sciences Course
